Mac N Cheese Pizza Recipe

Description

Macaroni And Cheese, referred to as Ma N Cheese by many, is a very popular meal in the United States! Combine it with another popular meal, Pizza, and you get Mac N Cheese Pizza, a meal that'll surely keep your taste buds entertained!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1.5 Cups Elbow Macaroni
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon All-Purpose Flour
  • Milk
  • 3/4 Pound Processed Cheese (Such As Velveeta), Cubed
  • 1/2 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 400 Degrees F or 200 Degrees C and spray some cooking spray onto a 10x15 inch baking sheet. Unroll the crush of a pizza and place onto a prepared baking pan.
  2. Add water to a large pot and slightly salt it. Boil this water and cook elbow macaroni in it, stirring occasionally until it is properly cooked but still firm. This should take around 8 minutes. Drain when done.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Cook and stir flour for around 2 minutes with butter until it achieves a slightly toasted fragrance. Whisk milk into this and simmer the mixture until thickened. Whisk constantly at this stage, it should take around another 2 minutes. Put the processed cheese cubes in and let them melt. Stir often and aim for a smooth cheese sauce.
  4. Spread around 3/4 cup of the cheese sauce onto the pizza crush and stir cooked macaroni into the remaining cheese sauce in the saucepan. Spoon the macaroni and cheese onto the pizza crush in an even layer. Sprink the top of the Pizza with Mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake until the crust is lightly browned and the Mozarella cheese is golden brown. It should take around 15 minutes.
